Do these values differ from the estimates from the first part of the assignment? What is the impact of imputing missing data on the estimates of the total daily number of steps?

Yes, they differ. Previously, missing values were ignored and, this way, they accounted for zero step. In the new dataset, missing values were replaced by the mean of the respective interval and, therefore, the number of total steps increased. More noticeably, the group ranging between 0 and 5000 steps has a lower frequency, while the group ranging between 10000 and 15000 has a higher frequency.
